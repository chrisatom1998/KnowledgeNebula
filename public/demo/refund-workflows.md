Refunds are handled by support staff through the admin dashboard. This document lists limits and approvals.

## Approval Limits
- **Support Representative**: Up to $50 refund permission.
- **Support Manager**: Up to $250 refund permission.
- **Finance Director**: Unlimited refund permission.

## Ledger Entries
All refund transactions are reconciled against PostgreSQL records.
