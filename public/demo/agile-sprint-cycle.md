We run two-week sprint cycles. This document details our agile planning activities.

## Sprint Ceremonies
- **Sprint Planning**: Every second Monday.
- **Daily Stand-ups**: 15 minutes, focus on blockers.
- **Backlog Refinement**: Weekly ticket reviews.
- **Sprint Retrospective**: End-of-sprint review.
