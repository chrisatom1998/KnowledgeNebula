When a customer files a dispute with their bank, Stripe triggers a dispute event.

## Action Steps
1. The subscription is set to **past_due** status.
2. Customer is notified via email.
3. Support has 7 days to compile evidence (logs, usage records) to submit to Stripe to fight the chargeback.
