This guide explains subscription status changes, trial durations, and automated user downgrades.

## Statuses
- **trialing**: 14-day trial, no credit card required.
- **active**: Account successfully billed.
- **past_due**: Payment failed; subscription enters a 15-day grace period (dunning).
- **canceled**: Services revoked, user data marked for deletion.
