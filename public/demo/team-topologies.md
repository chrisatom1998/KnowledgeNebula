We organize engineering teams around specific domains to maximize autonomy.

## Teams
- **Platform Team**: Owns cloud configuration, EKS, and CI/CD pipelines.
- **Billing Team**: Owns Stripe integrations, checkout pages, and analytics.
- **Core Product Team**: Owns frontend applications and APIs.
