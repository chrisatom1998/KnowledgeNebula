When a P0/P1 incident occurs, we conduct a post-mortem review to prevent recurrences.

## Guidelines
- **Blameless Culture**: Focus on system weaknesses, not operator errors.
- **Root Cause**: Use the "5 Whys" analysis.
- **Action Items**: Assign clear owners and deadlines.
